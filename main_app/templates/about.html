About Us


This is an app to register all the places, sightseeing that you've been to:

This is an app designed to register and organize all the places and sightseeing spots you’ve been to. Whether you’re a passionate traveler, a casual explorer, or someone who loves to look back on past adventures, this app helps you keep track of every destination in one convenient place. You can log your visits with details like location, date, photos, notes, and personal experiences. The app allows you to mark landmarks, museums, natural wonders, restaurants, and other points of interest you’ve explored, helping you build a complete travel history over time. One of the key features is the interactive map, where you can visually see all the places you've visited, making it easy to reflect on past journeys or share your travel story with friends and family. You can also categorize your visits by country, state, or type of location—such as cultural sites, nature trips, or city tours. The app includes search and filter options, so you can quickly find where you’ve been and what you’ve done. Beyond recording, the app can assist in planning future travel. Based on your travel history, it can suggest new destinations, nearby attractions you haven’t visited yet, or create bucket lists based on your interests. Whether it’s climbing a mountain, visiting UNESCO heritage sites, or simply relaxing on different beaches, the app helps you keep those goals visible and achievable. You can also personalize your travel log with custom notes, journal entries, and even rate the places you've visited. Add tags like “family trip,” “solo adventure,” or “honeymoon” to remember the purpose of each journey. The app also supports secure cloud backup, ensuring your travel memories are never lost. Overall, this app becomes your personal travel companion—part diary, part planner, and part inspiration board. It not only helps you stay organized but also motivates you to explore more, revisit special memories, and make the most out of every trip you take. Whether you're building your travel legacy or simply love to stay organized, this app makes it easy and enjoyable.

You can record your visits and plan your future travels:

You can record your visits and plan your future travels using a travel journal or a dedicated app. Documenting your trips allows you to capture memories, track visited destinations, and reflect on experiences like local culture, food, and people. Photos, notes, and location tags help you preserve special moments and revisit them anytime. At the same time, planning future travels becomes more efficient when you use these records. You can analyze where you've been, identify gaps in your travel map, and set goals for new destinations. Organizing your trips by category—such as adventure, relaxation, or historical—helps you build diverse travel experiences. Apps with features like interactive maps, itinerary builders, and weather tracking make it easier to plan ahead. You can also set budgets, book accommodations, and create checklists to ensure smooth travel. Whether you're a casual traveler or a passionate explorer, combining past travel logs with future planning gives a complete view of your journey and inspires meaningful adventures ahead.
